FAQ

Everything you wanted to know but were too busy charging to ask.

ChargeVia is what EV charging should have been from the start. Fast chargers, a real convenience store, driver's lounge, clean restrooms - the whole gas station experience, minus the gas. Pull in, charge up, grab a coffee, use a bathroom that won't haunt your dreams, and get back on the road.

Most charging networks stick chargers in parking lots or garages and call it a day. We actually thought about what makes charging suck less: multiple fast chargers so you're not waiting in line, a real convenience store, a lounge to hang out in, clean restrooms, and covered bays so you don't get rained on. It's a pit stop, not a punishment.

ChargeVia stations have CCS (Combined Charging System) connectors, which work with most electric vehicles including Tesla (with adapter), Rivian, Ford, Chevrolet, Hyundai, Kia, BMW, Mercedes, Volkswagen, and others. If your EV has a CCS port, it'll work at ChargeVia.

150-350kW DC fast charging. Translation: most EVs can add 100+ miles of range in about 15-20 minutes. Enough time for a bathroom break and a snack, not enough time to watch a movie.

Two ways: (1) Plug-and-go - register your vehicle once with Pathfinder Rewards, then just plug in and charging starts and bills automatically. No cards, no apps, no QR codes. (2) Credit/debit card - tap or insert any major card at the charger. No account required.

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network loyalty program for EV charging. Your membership works at ChargeVia and partner networks. You get flat-rate pricing, earn credits back on every charge, and can use those credits anywhere Pathfinder is accepted. The Explorer tier is free.
