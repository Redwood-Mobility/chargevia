ChargeVia is what EV charging should have been from the start. Fast chargers, a real convenience store, driver's lounge, clean restrooms - the whole gas station experience, minus the gas. Pull in, charge up, grab a coffee, use a bathroom that won't haunt your dreams, and get back on the road.

Most charging networks stick chargers in parking lots or garages and call it a day. We actually thought about what makes charging suck less: multiple fast chargers so you're not waiting in line, a real convenience store, a lounge to hang out in, clean restrooms, and covered bays so you don't get rained on.

ChargeVia stations have both CCS (Combined Charging System) and NACS (North American Charging Standard) connectors. CCS is the standard used by most EVs from brands like Hyundai, Kia, BMW, Mercedes, and Volkswagen. NACS is Tesla's connector that's quickly becoming the new industry standard - Ford, GM, Rivian, and others are all switching to it. Bottom line: if you drive an EV, you can charge at ChargeVia.

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network loyalty program for EV charging. Your membership works at ChargeVia and partner networks. You get flat-rate pricing, earn credits back on every charge, and can use those credits anywhere Pathfinder is accepted. The Explorer tier is free.
