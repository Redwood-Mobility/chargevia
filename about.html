About ChargeVia

We're building the EV gas station you actually want to stop at.

Our Mission

Make EV charging as convenient as filling up at your favorite gas station.

Let's be honest: most EV charging sucks. You drive to a parking garage, find half the chargers broken, wait in line, and hope there's a bathroom nearby. That's not convenience. That's a chore.

We looked at what makes a great gas station experience - fast fueling, clean restrooms, good snacks, in and out - and built that for EVs.

The result is ChargeVia: fast chargers plus an on-site convenience store at every location. Pull in, plug in, grab a coffee, use a clean restroom, and get back on the road.

What We Believe

Charging Should Be Easy

Not a treasure hunt for working chargers. Not an hour-long ordeal. Just plug in and go.

Convenience Matters

A parking lot with a charger isn't a destination. Real convenience means restrooms, snacks, and services.

Format Works

Gas stations figured this out decades ago. We're applying that proven model to the EV era.

Gas Station Heritage

Converting yesterday's gas stations into tomorrow's charging hubs.

Many of our ChargeVia locations are former gas stations. These are community landmarks - the corner station you've driven past for years. We're giving them new life with clean energy infrastructure.

It's sustainable repurposing: instead of tearing down and building new, we retrofit existing sites. The familiar format works - we're just swapping fossil fuels for electrons.

  • Existing infrastructure, new purpose
  • Familiar community locations
  • Sustainable approach to EV charging
  • Proven convenience format
