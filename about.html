About ChargeVia

The EV gas station you actually want to stop at.

What We're Building

The gas station format has worked for a century for a reason: it's convenient, quick, and provides what drivers need. We're taking that proven model and electrifying it.

Sometimes that means building new stations from scratch. Often, it means taking an old, abandoned service station and breathing new life into it. We keep the canopy, the convenience store, and the community feel—but swap the pumps for high-speed electrons.

Who's Behind This

ChargeVia is developed and operated by Altara Energy, an EV charging infrastructure company focused on building out charging networks across the American West.

Altara brings the engineering expertise, site development know-how, and operational chops to make ChargeVia stations run smoothly. We handle the electrons—you enjoy the experience.

Part of Pathfinder Rewards

ChargeVia is a proud member of the Pathfinder Rewards network. That means your ChargeVia visits earn you points you can use across all participating charging networks.

One account. Multiple networks. Real rewards. No complicated math.
