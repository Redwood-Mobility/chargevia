Questions & Answers

Everything you wanted to know but were too busy charging to ask.

What is ChargeVia?

ChargeVia is what EV charging should have been from the start. Fast chargers, a real convenience store, driver's lounge, clean restrooms - the whole gas station experience, minus the gas. Pull in, charge up, grab a coffee, use a bathroom that won't haunt your dreams, and get back on the road.

How is ChargeVia different?

Most charging networks stick chargers in dark parking lots or garages and call it a day. We actually thought about the human driving the car. We offer multiple fast chargers (no lines), a real store (snacks!), a lounge (Wi-Fi!), and covered bays (dry!).

What vehicles can charge here?

If it has a plug, we probably charge it. ChargeVia stations feature both CCS (Combined Charging System) and NACS (Tesla style) connectors. Whether you drive a Ford, Tesla, Hyundai, Rivian, or something else, we've got you covered.

How fast is the charging?

Fast. We use 150-350kW DC fast chargers. In plain English: most EVs can add 100+ miles of range in about 15-20 minutes. Just enough time for a pit stop.

How do I pay?

Option 1 (Best): Join Pathfinder Rewards. Plug in, and the car talks to the charger to handle payment automatically.

Option 2: Credit/Debit card. Tap or insert at the machine. No app required.

What is Pathfinder Rewards?

It's our loyalty network. It works at ChargeVia and our partner networks. You get flat-rate pricing (no weird surge pricing), earn credits back on every charge, and access to the seamless "Plug & Charge" experience. Learn more here.

Do you buy old gas stations?

Yes! We love giving old stations new life. If you have a site you think would work, let us know.
