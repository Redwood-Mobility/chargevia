CCS (Combined Charging System) has been the standard for most non-Tesla EVs in North America. It's a larger connector that combines AC and DC charging.

NACS (North American Charging Standard) is Tesla's connector design, now being adopted by most major automakers. It's smaller and more ergonomic.

Don't worry about which one you need - ChargeVia stations have both, and we'll have adapters available if you need them.
