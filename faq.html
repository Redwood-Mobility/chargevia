About ChargeVia

ChargeVia is what EV charging should have been from the start. Fast chargers, a real convenience store, driver's lounge, clean restrooms - the whole gas station experience, minus the gas. Pull in, charge up, grab a coffee, use a bathroom that won't haunt your dreams, and get back on the road.

Most charging networks stick chargers in parking lots or garages and call it a day. We actually thought about what makes charging suck less: multiple fast chargers so you're not waiting in line, a real convenience store, a lounge to hang out in, clean restrooms, and covered bays so you don't get rained on. It's a pit stop, not a punishment.

We're building stations in cities and suburbs where EV drivers actually are - along commute routes, near shopping and dining, and accessible from major highways. Some are converted gas stations, others are new builds - all follow the familiar gas station format. Check our Locations page for updates and to join the waitlist for your area.

Charging

ChargeVia stations have CCS (Combined Charging System) connectors, which work with most electric vehicles including Tesla (with adapter), Rivian, Ford, Chevrolet, Hyundai, Kia, BMW, Mercedes, Volkswagen, and others. If your EV has a CCS port, it'll work at ChargeVia.

150-350kW DC fast charging. Translation: most EVs can add 100+ miles of range in about 15-20 minutes. Enough time for a bathroom break and a snack, not enough time to watch a movie.

Two ways: (1) Plug-and-go - register your vehicle once with Pathfinder Rewards, then just plug in and charging starts and bills automatically. No cards, no apps, no QR codes. (2) Credit/debit card - tap or insert any major card at the charger. No account required for credit card payments.

Pricing varies by location but is always flat-rate - you pay the same per kWh regardless of time of day. Pathfinder Rewards members get the best rates plus credits back on every charge. Check the station display for current pricing.

Pathfinder Rewards

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network loyalty program for EV charging. Your membership works at ChargeVia and partner networks. You get flat-rate pricing, earn credits back on every charge, and can use those credits anywhere Pathfinder is accepted.

Yep. The Explorer tier costs you exactly nothing. No membership fee, no hidden charges. You just pay for electricity. Higher tiers (Voyager, Pioneer, Trailblazer) are earned by charging, not by paying more fees.
